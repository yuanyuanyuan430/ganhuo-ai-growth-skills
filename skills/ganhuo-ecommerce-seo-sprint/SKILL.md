# 干活 电商 SEO 冲刺

## Overview

Use this skill to turn ecommerce SEO into a staged operating plan instead of a pile of article ideas.

## Workflow

1. Audit current baseline: traffic, indexed pages, category structure, product feed, content, backlinks, conversion, and tracking.
2. Months 0-3: fix foundations, keyword/category mapping, internal links, basic content, and safe early links.
3. Months 3-6: diagnose volatility, expand content clusters, add forum/community signals, and improve product/category pages.
4. Months 6-9: strengthen CRO, comparison/review pages, templates, and content-to-product paths.
5. Months 9-12: build linkable assets, industry reports, PR, expert quotes, review strategy, and brand search demand.
6. Set sprint metrics: impressions, ranking buckets, indexed pages, organic revenue, assisted conversions, link quality, and content decay.

## Output

Return a 12-month roadmap with sprint goals, page types, link/PR assets, CRO tasks, and measurement gates.

- Do not sell SEO growth as linear or guaranteed.
- Do not recommend low-quality link buying, copied reviews, or fake expertise.
