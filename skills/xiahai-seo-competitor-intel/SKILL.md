# 下海 SEO 竞品情报

## Overview

Use this skill to pick the right competitors for the question instead of copying the loudest site in the niche.

## Workflow

1. Clarify the research question: SEO traffic, conversion, monetization, product positioning, content system, or community acquisition.
2. Build competitor sets: direct competitors, SERP competitors, industry leaders, indirect substitutes, and cross-category references.
3. For each competitor, inspect page types, keyword clusters, content depth, linkable assets, conversion paths, brand trust signals, and community/UGC presence.
4. Separate what to copy structurally from what to avoid or reinterpret.
5. Score opportunities by feasibility, differentiation, content gap, linkability, and time to impact.
6. Turn findings into page ideas, experiments, and no-go warnings.

## Output

Return a competitor matrix plus opportunity map: what to learn, what to ignore, and what to test first.

## References

Read `references/source-map.md` when you need source provenance, material-specific guardrails, or the original file list behind this skill.

## Boundaries

- Do not infer private traffic or revenue as fact; label estimates.
- Do not recommend copying copy, design, or brand claims.
